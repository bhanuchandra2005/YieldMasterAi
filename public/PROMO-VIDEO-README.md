# Promo Video (Logo Click Modal)

Add your **8–12 second** cinematic promo video here so it plays when users click the YieldMasterAI logo on the landing page.

## File to add

- **Required:** `promo-video.mp4` (MP4, 8–12 seconds)
- **Optional:** `promo-video.webm` (WebM for better Safari support)

## Suggested content (brief)

- Aerial drone shot over a vast green field
- Golden sunrise / morning light, soft fog
- Optional: baked-in overlays for crop health %, yield prediction, moisture
- Style: realistic, 4K, smart farming / AI agriculture

## Where to get the video

- **Stock footage:** Pexels, Pixabay, Shutterstock (search: “drone agricultural field”, “farm aerial sunrise”)
- **AI video:** Runway, Pika, or similar tools using your cinematic description
- **Edit:** Add HUD-style graphics (crop health, yield %, moisture) in Premiere, DaVinci Resolve, or After Effects if not baked in

Once `promo-video.mp4` is in this `public` folder, the logo modal will play it automatically with the built-in AI-style overlay (Crop health, Moisture, Yield prediction).
